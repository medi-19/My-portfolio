Who I Am

Hi, I’m Devd Medi, a dedicated Front-End Developer with over 3 years of professional experience, coding since 2021. I build responsive, interactive, and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.

Over the years, I’ve worked extensively with Bootstrap to create clean, modern interfaces and am currently transitioning to Tailwind CSS and React.js to enhance my development workflow. My goal is to master the MERN Stack and eventually expand into software development with C# (.NET) and mobile app development using React Native or Flutter.

Beyond coding, I have experience in content writing, data entry, social media marketing, computer operation, and graphic design with Canva — skills that help me see web projects from both a technical and creative perspective.

My Mission

To create seamless, high-performing digital experiences that combine design precision, clean code, and real-world functionality. I aim to build meaningful web applications that not only look great but also solve real problems and deliver measurable impact.

My Motivation

Technology evolves every day — and that constant evolution is what drives me. I’m motivated by learning something new, improving my craft, and pushing myself to turn imagination into innovation. Every challenge in development is an opportunity to grow, and I take pride in solving problems that inspire creativity and progress.
